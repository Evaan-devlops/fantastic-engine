Keep Terminal 1 running:

Watching for commands. Press Ctrl+C to stop.

Terminal 2 — validate and submit

First ensure the corrected TaskIntent is saved, then run:

INTENT_FILE="$(ls -t SOP/generated/*_task_intent.json | head -n 1)"

.venv/bin/python -m sop_automation task validate-intent \
  --intent-file "$INTENT_FILE"

Expected:

Intent validation: PASS

Then submit:

.venv/bin/python -m sop_automation task submit \
  --intent-file "$INTENT_FILE"

Expected:

STARTED
Run ID: <actual-run-id>

Save it:

RUN_ID="<paste-the-actual-run-id>"
